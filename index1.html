CommonPitfalls

Voltage Mismatch

Connecting 5V components directly to a 3.3V ESP32 will destroy the board.

No Common Ground

Using separate batteries? Connect GND pins together or signals won't work.

BT Connection

HC-05 is tricky. Verify baud rates (9600 vs 38400) and RX/TX wiring.

Missing Libraries

Code failing? 90% of the time you forgot to install the specific library.

Cost Reduction & Smart Planning

Student-friendly tips to save money, avoid mistakes, and plan projects smartly.

Before Buying Components

Study requirements: Define exactly what your project needs before shopping.
Don't copy blindly: Verify tutorials and avoid extra parts.
Check availability: Some online components may not be available locally.
Buy only needed parts: Avoid mega-kits; buy what the project requires.

Power & Battery Awareness

Check spec: Verify voltage and current requirements before buying power sources.
Avoid random batteries: Don't use mismatched or extra batteries without checking ratings.
Know cell types: Rechargeable vs non-rechargeable â€” use appropriate cells.
Use correct charger: Always use the recommended charger for rechargeable batteries.
Wrong supply risks: Incorrect power can damage components and waste money.
